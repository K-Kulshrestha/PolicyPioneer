Professional Liability Insurance (Errors and Omissions Insurance)

Professional Liability Insurance, also known as errors and omissions insurance, helps protect your business if you are sued for negligence in the performance of services you provide to your customers.

Covers/Protections Against:

  • Actual or alleged acts or errors
  • Libel and slander allegations arising out of your professional services
  • Cost of legal defense for covered liability claims

Cost:

Several factors determine the cost, but we at Policy Pioneer quickly provide you estimates on our Home page.

Do I need it?

General liability on your business policy covers mistakes or errors. But unless your policy includes professional liability coverage, you’re probably not covered for things like botching a customer’s dye job, making errors on a client’s tax returns or misdiagnosing Fido’s upset tummy.
